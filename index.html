The source of inspiration

I got my desire to become a Full Stack Developer after working with e-commerce in a small bussiness that sold new and used video games.

The games were sold on multiple online marketplaces with a wide target audience. The company also had a small webshop that I was administrating on a smaller level. While working on the webshop something sparked within me and the time just flew away. Within a couple of days work I realised that I had found something that I could be happy working with. Building something from the ground up that could generate a steady income was amazing to me.

I signed up for studying a couple more courses to be able to fulfill the admission requirements for a higher vocational education to further delve into the world of programming in both back- and frontend development.

I am currently studying to become a Full Stack .Net Developer at Chas Academy, Stockholm.
